The practice

A direction becomes a practice. A practice becomes a body of work. A body of work becomes a company. Over time, the company becomes part of the life it was meant to serve.

01
Begin with what matters

Choose work that can belong to more than one part of your life.

02
Make progress small enough to repeat

Let useful movement today become the material of a larger future.

03
Build capacity around the vision

Add the people, systems, and judgment that let the work grow without consuming the life around it.

04
Let contribution compound

Keep a memory of what people give so value can remember how it was created.

The next chapter
